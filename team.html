Careers at SparkDojo

Work, Create, Grow

About us

Our members are passionate, driven, self-motivated individuals who enjoy working in a creative environment where we discuss, share opinions, and pursue new business opportunities openly. We are a diverse team of experienced bilinguals and native English speakers from a variety of professional and academic backgrounds.

Benefits

At SparkDojo we aim to create a work environment which inspires creativity, drives growth, and supports personal development. Team member responsibilities include a broad range of areas such as education, program development, sales, marketing, and administration among others. Our flexible work environment encourages staff members to take on roles in multiple areas according to their own strengths and interests.

If invited to become a full time staff member, we offer visa sponsorship, paid transportation, an open vacation policy and competitive salary.

How to apply

We are always looking for new team members, if you are interested in working with us, please send us your resume and cover letter by emailing sparkdojo.support@onedrops.com
